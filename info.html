Terms & Conditions

By engaging Tunde Visuals for photography or videography services, you agree to the following terms. These terms govern the relationship between the client and Tunde Visuals and are designed to ensure clarity and professionalism for every project.

Booking & Confirmation

A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total package price is required to secure your booking. Your date is not confirmed until the deposit is received and a confirmation invoice has been sent. The remaining balance is due on or before the day of the shoot unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Client Conduct

Clients are expected to cooperate with the creative direction provided by Tunde Visuals during the session. Any behaviour that is abusive, discriminatory, or unsafe may result in immediate termination of the session with no refund issued.

Deliverables

Edited photo and/or video deliverables will be provided within the timeframe specified in your package agreement. Standard turnaround is 7–14 business days for photos and 2–4 weeks for video projects. Rush delivery may be available at an additional cost.

Usage Rights

Tunde Visuals retains the right to use any photographs or video footage captured during the session for portfolio, website, social media, and marketing purposes unless a written agreement for exclusivity has been signed prior to the shoot. The client receives a personal usage license for the delivered media.

Cancellation by Client

If you cancel more than 14 days before the scheduled date, the deposit is forfeited but you will not be charged the remaining balance. If you cancel within 14 days, the full package amount is due. Tunde Visuals reserves the right to reschedule due to extreme weather, illness, or circumstances beyond our control, in which case the deposit will be transferred to the new date.

Limitation of Liability

Tunde Visuals shall not be held liable for any incidental or consequential damages arising from the use of deliverables. In the unlikely event of technical failure resulting in total loss of footage or images, liability is limited to a full refund of fees paid for that specific project.

Security

Your privacy and data security are important to us. Here is how Tunde Visuals handles your information:

  • Payment Security: All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant gateways. We do not store or have access to your full payment card details.
  • Data Storage: Your personal information (name, email, phone number, event details) is stored securely and used only for the purpose of delivering your project and communicating with you.
  • Image & Video Privacy: Raw files and unedited media are kept confidential. Only the final edited deliverables are used for portfolio or promotional purposes, and you may request otherwise in writing.
  • Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to deliver the service (e.g., printing labs, album manufacturers) or as required by law.
  • Website Security: Our website uses HTTPS encryption and security headers to protect your data while browsing. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely via email.
  • Data Retention: We retain your project files and personal information for up to 12 months after project completion, after which they are securely deleted unless otherwise agreed.

How to Book

Booking a session with Tunde Visuals is simple. Follow the steps below:

  1. Choose Your Package — Browse our services on the Services page to find the package that fits your needs. We offer photography, videography, and combo packages.
  2. Check Availability — Contact us via the contact form, email at Tundevisuals@gmail.com, or send a direct message on Instagram to confirm your preferred date.
  3. Secure Your Date — Pay the 50% deposit via bank transfer or the payment link we send you. Your booking is confirmed once the deposit clears and you receive a confirmation invoice.
  4. Pre-Shoot Consultation — We will schedule a quick call or chat to discuss your vision, preferred style, location, outfits, and any special requests so we are fully prepared.
  5. Shoot Day — Show up and let us handle the rest. We will guide you through poses, lighting, and direction to ensure the best results.
  6. Receive Your Media — Your edited photos or video will be delivered digitally via a private gallery link within the agreed timeframe. You can download, share, and enjoy your content.

Refund Policy

Tunde Visuals strives for 100% client satisfaction. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Deposit: The 50% booking deposit is non-refundable. It covers administrative costs, scheduling, and the time reserved exclusively for your project.
  • Balance Payment: The remaining 50% is due on or before the shoot date. Once paid, this amount is refundable only if Tunde Visuals cancels the session due to circumstances on our end (e.g., equipment failure, illness).
  • Dissatisfaction with Deliverables: If you are not satisfied with the final deliverables, we offer one round of revisions at no additional cost. If after revisions you remain unsatisfied, a partial refund of up to 30% of the balance paid may be issued at our discretion, provided the work delivered meets the technical and creative standards outlined in your package.
  • Rescheduling: If you need to reschedule, the deposit transfers to the new date. No additional fees apply if notice is given at least 7 days before the shoot. Rescheduling within 7 days may incur a small rebooking fee.
  • Force Majeure: In the event of natural disaster, government restrictions, or other events beyond our control, both parties agree to reschedule. No refunds are issued for force majeure cancellations; the deposit and any payments will be applied to a rescheduled date.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

Tunde Visuals is based in Nigeria and serves clients across the country. We are also available for international projects — travel fees may apply depending on location.

How long does it take to receive my photos or video?

Photo editing typically takes 7–14 business days. Video projects (editing, color grading, sound design) take 2–4 weeks, depending on the complexity. Rush options are available upon request.

Do you provide RAW or unedited files?

We deliver fully edited, color-graded final products. RAW and unedited files are not included in standard packages unless specifically negotiated. This ensures that only our best work represents your brand or event.

Can I request specific edits or retouching?

Absolutely. We offer one round of revisions on all deliverables. Additional retouching or specific edit requests beyond the first revision may incur extra charges depending on the scope.

How do I receive my final files?

All deliverables are delivered via a private online gallery link. You can download high-resolution files directly. Video projects are delivered via secure cloud storage or Google Drive.

What happens if it rains on my shoot day?

We monitor weather closely. If conditions are unsafe or will significantly affect quality, we will work with you to reschedule at no cost. Deposits transfer automatically to the new date.

Do you work with a team or solo?

Tunde Visuals operates with a core team of experienced professionals. Depending on the project scope, we may bring additional shooters, lighting assistants, or editors to ensure the highest quality result.

How do I pay? What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and secure online payments. A payment link will be sent to you after booking. All major debit and credit cards are supported through our payment gateway.
